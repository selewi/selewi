Available for:

Details

Developed for an ISP, Inclima stands for Internet Client Management and allows payment tracking through a web responsive UI. After some meetings, the desired requirements were splitted and supplied in three iterations:

The first one covered most of the data-entry that the ISP had to about clients and subscriptions.

  • Users, roles & authentification
  • Client management
    • Google maps support to localize client address
  • Subscriptions management

The second iteration was mostly back-end and went into production two months later once most of the required data to work and process were fully loaded into the database.

  • Client payment
  • Automated subscription charges for debtors
  • Overview, statistics & reports

Finally, the last iteration was finished two months later, before subscription cut deadline.

  • Automated subscription cut for debtors
  • System parametrization
  • Updated overview, statistics & reports

The design was fully documented as well as the code for future maintenance. At the end of every iteration, the software was hosted and the ISP employees were trained to operate it.
