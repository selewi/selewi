Name: Itsy Bitsy Spider

Length: 2 weeks

Team size: 2

Roles: Programmer

Tools used: Unity engine, Visual Studio 2017

Available for:

Details

The client, who also was the artist, needed to create a simple 3D app about a children cartoon song. Some of the satisfied requirements were:

  • Orbital camera and parallax effect using the device accelerometer
  • Environment interactions on device shake
  • Weather changes including particles, lighting effects and wind blows that affect the plants
  • Asset & animation integrations
  • Music integration
  • Texture optimization for mobile/tablets
