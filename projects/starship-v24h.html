Details

This was my first published game, It's called Starship v24h because it was meant to be finished in under 24 hours, I put myself this challenge because before this project I couldn't finish anything I made. I must admit it was a good idea to try to develop an entire game under these conditions, all the assets, music, coding, and design were made from zero once the timer started.

For my surprise, at 16 hours I had a finished product, a simple but complete retro arcade space shooter. Before this game I did Starship in under 12 hours with a 'Space Invaders' concept but it has never been published.
