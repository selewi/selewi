Available for:

Details

Fueled by horror and nostalgia, Rottenwood Lake will take you to a swamp where some cult activity is going on. The game was made for the Haunted PS1 gamejam and with a world-wide team, pulling a game like this in a short period of time was a huge challenge.

The game length is about 30 to 60 minutes, featuring a complete narrative story, old retro graphics and many mechanics to entertain the player while riding a boat and getting lost in Rottenwood Lake. In order to win the game the player must escape from the swamps by collecting some artifacts around the place, but of course there's a plot, a horrible creature is around the place...
