Details

I didn't know it back then, but I could make games aboard a train! This game was developed for the Argentina train jam on a trip from Buenos Aires to Córdoba that took 18 hours, there were many teams to join in but for some reason I wanted to challenge myself to work alone under these weird conditions that train jam put me in.

Considering I didn't have much time and I had to make all the assets and audio, I decided to go for a simple 2 players game which involves a cat, a cookie, and a lot of hungry rats.
