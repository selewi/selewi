About me

Engines, IDE's & DB's

I use Unity + Visual Studio to make 2D and 3D video games, I like creating my own custom tools to improve workflow, also I have knowledge in particle systems, mecanim, shader implementation, scriptable objects and audio implementation with FMOD studio plugin!

Team friendly

If I need to work with someone else, I'm also up to it... I've managed teams and worked with people from all around the world remotely and also sharing a place in an office. I'm used to work with many team communication tools and version control (SVN/GIT).
